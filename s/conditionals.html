Conditionals

Complete the sentences:

  1. I don't know the answer. If I (know) the answer, I'd tell you.
  2. I have a car. I couldn't travel very much if I (not/have) a car.
  3. I don't want to go out. If I (want) to go out, I'd go.
  4. We don't have a key. If we (have) a key, we could get into the house.
  5. I'm not hungry. If I (be) hungry, I would eat something.
  6. She enjoys her work. She wouldn't do it if she (not/enjoy) it.

Put the verb in the correct form:

  1. If he (have) the money, he would buy a fast car.
  2. Jane likes living in a city. She (not/be) happy if she lived in the country.
  3. If I wanted to learn Italian, I (go) to Italy.
  4. What would you do if you (win) a lot of money?

Complete the sentences. Choose from the box:

  1. I'd buy that jacket if it (be/a bit cheaper).
  2. If there was a good film on TV tonight, I (watch) it.
